Purpose of the Site:

The purpose of this site is to give Magic: The Gathering players a resource where they can get information about many aspects of the game. Players can learn general facts about the game, learn how play, look for cards, and build their own decks.


About The Game

Magic: The Gathering is a trading card game that was created in 1993 by a man named Richard Garfield. It has the unique distinction of being the first trading card game (TCG) ever created. As of 2015, Magic has roughly 12 million players world-wide. It can be played by two or more players in many different formats which fall into two categories: Constructed and Limited.


Objective

The goal of Magic: The Gathering is to be the last player standing. This can be accomplished in a variety of ways, but the most common way is to reduce your opponent's life from 20 down to 0.
