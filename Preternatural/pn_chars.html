Main Characters:

Jason Gray

Jason is an 18-year-old high school student who is struggling to keep his newly-manifested powers under control. He has spent the last couple of months believing that he has dissociative identity disorder and that his "other half" doing things without him knowing it. Because of his behavior, many of the other students at his school have ostracized him. After meeting with Luke, he realizes that he isn't crazy and see his new powers as a way to turn his life around.

Collin Gray

Collin is Jason's 16-year-old brother. Unlike Jason, he's more popular in school. Because of his popularity and self-confidence, he is more comfortable being reckless. He's one of the best players on the soccer team.

Hartley Sinclair
Hartley is a 20-year-old girl who ran away from home after her powers manifested themselves. Neither of her parents have powers so she ran to prevent her parents from being caught in the crossfire.

Luke Flynn
Luke is a 29-year-old man who worked for the WPN. He was a prodigy and had the added advantage of having his powers manifest on his 13th birthday. After a decade of working for the WPN, he went rogue and has been trying to take down the WPN ever since. He is now the leader of the Preterhuman Reformation.

Sasha Valentine
Sasha is a 27-year old woman who accidentally injured someone when transitioning between development stages. Feeling guilty, she went to go see the her victim in the hospital to apologize. After Sasha left, he mysteriously died and because she was the last person to see him alive, she was the Prime Suspect.
